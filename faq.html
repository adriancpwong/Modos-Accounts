GENERAL

How do we have so many accounts?

We invest a lot of money into a supplier, who purchases these accounts from multiple different sellers and we mass purchase them. We then resell them for a slight profit.

How safe are these accounts?

About 2 in 100 accounts are recovered. This is because, the people who sell these accounts to us are scammers, who have either recovered the account after we paid them money or have obtained the account illegally. In both cases, we provide full replacements, either in the form of store credit, or by offering you an account up to 50% more valuable that your previous.

Do you offer refunds?

In most cases, we usually only provide replacements. However, in extreme cases, for example, if an account is banned by Riot a day after the purchase, we will offer a full refund.

How long does a warrantee last?

Ranging from 1 month to 1 year depending on the account. Please specifically ask about this through Skype or email. You can assume all accounts listed here have a one month warrantee.

PURCHASING

How do we purchase an account?

Simply click the “Buy Now” button next to the account you want. The account will be set to your PayPal email, so make sure you have access to it.

How will an account be delivered?

We will set the account to the PayPal email you used to purchase the account. If you would like the account set to another email address, please contact us on Skype and inform us there. You will get a confirmation email sent to your email address from, which will contain the username of the account. The password can then be recovered from the website.

What kind of payment do you accept?

Only PayPal.

How long will it take to deliver an account?

Up to 24 hours. If it takes longer, you are entitled to a full refund.
